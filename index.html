Executive Summary

Out of 328 coffee shops in Tangerang Selatan, 298 outlets (90%) are actionable retail acquisition targets—comprising 292 independent MSMEs and 6 local chain outlets (Suksestama, H47, Pilona). Only 30 national corporate chain outlets (Starbucks, Tomoro, Kopi Kenangan, Tuku, Fore, Flash, Nako, Bagi Kopi, Kopi Calf) are excluded from the scope as they fall under corporate banking. The growth of Tangsel coffee MSMEs is massive: +108 new outlets since 2023, indicating a wave of entrepreneurs representing a prime expansion opportunity for the banking ecosystem.

🎯 Acquisition Focus: Retail MSMEs

  • Addressable Market: 298 outlets spread across all sub-districts. Top three highest density zones: Serpong, Pamulang, and Pondok Aren—each serving as a priority target for a blitz acquisition campaign.
  • Local Chains = Quick Wins: 6 outlets from 3 local brands (Suksestama, H47, Pilona) require multi-location financial management but are not yet at corporate scale. This is a high-potential segment to onboard as flagship merchants—acquiring one brand secures multiple outlets simultaneously.
  • Why MSMEs First: The majority of F&B MSMEs do not yet have separate business accounts. Every new account opened represents net new funding for the bank.

💰 Priority #1: CASA Growth

  • Onboarding Strategy: Every acquired coffee MSME must be facilitated in opening a Livin' by Mandiri account as their primary operational account. By acquiring all target merchants, the total low-cost funding float reaches Rp852 million - Rp1.99 billion/day.
  • Livin' Merchant for Operations: Every Livin' account is instantly activated with Livin' Merchant, enabling shop owners to accept QRIS payments, monitor transactions in real-time, and enjoy automated settlements—making Mandiri their primary bank for daily operations.

*Note: Float/CASA estimates are dynamically calculated from the aggregation of (Average Price × Operating Hours × Base Transactions) × Crowd Multiplier (a 0.5x to 3.5x multiplier validated by Google Reviews volume for each shop).

📊 Fee-Based Income & Activation KPI

  • FBI Projection: 298 merchants × 0.7% MDR × Rp5 million daily digital volume = potential fee-based income of ~Rp312 million/month from the Tangsel cluster. This figure excludes transfer fees, top-ups, and other value-added services.
  • 2% Activation Target: For every batch of onboarded merchants, at least 2% must achieve Active Transacting Merchant status (≥10 trx/day) within the first 90 days. This metric serves as a leading indicator of successful active CASA conversion and long-term retention.

📱 Mandiri Digital Ecosystem

  • Full-Stack Onboarding: A single acquisition visit = Livin' account opening + Livin' Merchant activation + QRIS registration. This one-stop approach minimizes drop-off and immediately locks the merchant into the Mandiri ecosystem.
  • Cross-Sell Opportunity: Daily transaction data from Livin' Merchant acts as alternative credit scoring material. Coffee MSMEs with a consistent track record of ≥6 months are highly eligible for Micro Credit (KUR) or working capital loans for business expansion.
